Approach

Most teams grow output by adding people. The ratio barely moves — the system is the multiplier.

1

Find the bottleneck

Manual work, tribal knowledge, headcount pressure — the constraint is usually one process everyone has learned to live with.

2

Redesign the workflow end-to-end

Not a patch on top — the process rebuilt from first principles, designed, automated, measured.

3

Automate what shouldn’t need a human

AI and automation where they earn their place. The result is boring in the best way: fewer bottlenecks, faster throughput, a calmer company.

Same headcount, more output — that’s the whole trade.
